## Modifications on Daily Routines

- The uninstallation logic was optimized.
- Some dependencies have been upgraded.
- The `.ttc` font format is now supported.
- The drawing logic and drawing effect while previewing a font in the settings were optimized.

## New modules

### Magnified Dye Preview [UI_Optimization]

Provide magnified dye previews in related interfaces for better visibility and details.

### 随机任务：指导者任务记录上传助手 [Assistance]

配置完成后, 当进入“随机任务：指导者”任务副本并完成时, 自动记录并上传相关记录数据至 DungeonLogger 网站

![DungeonLoggerUploader](/assets/Changelog/1.9.0.0/DungeonLoggerUploader.png)

## Module modifications

### Quick World Travel Command [System]

- Now all the tasks related to login will be terminated after a successful login in order to prevent getting stuck.

### Auto Retainer Work [UI_Operation]

- Now the gap between the previous price and the current price will be provided in the notification after adjusting the price of an item for sale.
- Now the floating window will be hidden if there's nothing for sale at the moment.

### Alphascape V3.0 Helper [Assistance]

- The overall performance while executing the duty action was improved.

### Better Duty Finder Settings [UI_Optimization]

- The appearance of buttons and the display logic of texts were optimized based on the new changes introduced in Patch 7.35.

### Better Enmity List [UI_Optimization]

- New configuration `Show Cast Info`. Enabled by default.
- The drawing effect of the configuration interface was optimized.

### Auto FATE Level Sync [Combat]

- The performance was improved.
